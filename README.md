# 📊 Shop Sales Tracker

A simple, easy-to-use web application for tracking daily sales in your shop.

## Features

✨ **Easy Item Entry** - Quickly add items you sell with quantity and price

📊 **Daily Statistics** - See total items sold and revenue at a glance

💾 **Auto-Save** - Your data is automatically saved in your browser

📈 **Sales History** - Track your sales history across multiple days

📥 **Export Data** - Export your daily sales as a CSV file for record-keeping

📱 **Responsive Design** - Works great on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices

🔐 **User Accounts** - Create accounts and login from different devices

☁️ **Cloud Sync** - Optional cloud backup for your sales data

## How to Use

1. **Create Account** - Sign up with a username and password
2. **Add items** - Fill in the item name, quantity sold, and price per item
3. **Click "Add Item"** - Your sale will be added to today's list
4. **View summary** - See your total items sold and total revenue
5. **Delete items** - Click the "Delete" button to remove an incorrect entry
6. **Clear daily sales** - Click "Clear All Sales" to finish the day (saves to history)
7. **Export data** - Click "Export as CSV" to download your sales report

## Tracking Your Shop Inventory

### Record Products
- **Product Name** - Enter what you bought (Rice, Sugar, Oil, etc.)
- **Purchase Price** - How much you paid for the product
- **Quantity/Amount** - How much you have (in kg, liters, pieces, etc.)
- **Purchase Date** - When you bought the product

### Monitor Stock Levels
- **In Stock** - Products with quantity > 5 units
- **Low Stock** - Products with 1-5 units remaining
- **Out of Stock** - Products with 0 units

### Track Remaining Amounts
- View all quantities in the inventory table
- Update quantities as you sell items
- Automatic total value calculation for your inventory
- Real-time status updates

## Data Storage

### Local Storage
- All data is stored **locally in your browser** using localStorage
- No internet connection is required to use the app
- Data persists between browser sessions
- Each new day automatically starts fresh while previous days are kept in history

### Cloud Sync (Optional)
- If you login with cloud authentication, your data syncs automatically
- Access the same inventory from multiple devices
- Cloud backup is optional and falls back to local storage
